## Recording Tips
- Use screen recording software (OBS, Loom, or built-in screen recorder)
- Record in 1080p resolution
- Enable mouse highlighting
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Keep total length 5-7 minutes
- Add captions if possible
- Export as MP4 or upload to YouTube (unlisted)
